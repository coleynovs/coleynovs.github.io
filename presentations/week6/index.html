I think this wireframe works effectively for this type of business for several reasons. First, the user has the option to lease a car twice befor they even hit the images and content in the body. Once in the navigation bar and once clearly laid out before the body content starts. The search bar will lead to an easy leasing process to make it easier for users to spend money. Second the home page content is cars that are available for leasing so the user doesn't need to search through the website just to find the cars available.
