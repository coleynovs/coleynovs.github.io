Logo Inspiration

How the logo shows what the company is about

The logo shows what the company is about because the parrot is eating chocolate. It has chocolate around its mouth and on it’s finger tips. The parrot has a trickle of saliva dripping out of it’s mouth signifies that the chocolate is delicious.

Why the logo is effective for the brand

The logo is colourful, cute, rememberable and it provides the message that the logo is for a chocolate company.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out

The design is unique. People will find it funny and quirky and they will go to your chocolate bars because of the bright, colourful design!
